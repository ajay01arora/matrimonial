MESSAGE FROM FOUNDER


It is a matter of pride and immense pleasure that destiny has given me the opportunity to initiate something like this website for welfare of Gurjar Community. People often say that marriages are made in heaven, and maybe they are. But the important thing is for us to keep them intact here. We wish to play cupid in introducing you with your best friend and partner for life.

I would like to appreciate Gurjar Mahasabha Faridabad, Haryana for encouraging me to do so and for being the constant source of positivity throughout. Further I would like to thank some of my friends namely Sh. Tilak Raj Ji Baisla, Sh. Ramphool Singh Ji Bhati, Sh. Satender Ji Phagna, Sh. Sudershan Ji Nagar, Sh. Vijay Ji Nagar, Sh. Satish Ji Chandila, Sh. Sanjay Ji Bhadana, Sh. Satya Prakash Ji Bhadana, Sh. Yogender Ji Khatana and my niece Tanya Chauhan for supporting me time to time and for providing me with their valuable suggestions to develop and design this website.

I take this opportunity to compliment the whole dedicated team for their willing cooperation to the community at large. I wish them all the best for this endeavour of them.

I eagerly look forward to the suggestions from the community and website users for improving the content of this website.


Raj Kumar Choudhary
